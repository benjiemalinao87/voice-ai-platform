## FAQ

### For Sales Team

**Q: Will I get cold-called all day?**
> No! You only receive calls when a customer has expressed buying intent. These are warm, qualified leads.

**Q: What if I'm on another call?**
> The system will try the next agent on the list. You won't be interrupted.

**Q: What information do I get before connecting?**
> You'll hear a brief announcement with the reason for transfer (e.g., "Customer interested in window quote").

### For Marketing

**Q: How is this different from competitors?**
> Most systems require manual monitoring. Ours is fully automated with intelligent retry logic.

**Q: What's the elevator pitch?**
> "AI answers, qualifies, and automatically connects hot leads to your sales teamâ€”24/7, no human monitoring required."

### For Developers

**Q: How does the AI know when to transfer?**
> The AI has a function tool called `transfer_to_sales()`. When it detects buying intent, it calls this function.

**Q: What happens if Twilio is down?**
> The AI will receive a failure response and continue handling the call, offering to schedule a callback.

**Q: Can we customize the detection logic?**
> Yes! The AI's prompt determines when it calls the function. You can adjust the prompt for different triggers.
