## â“ FAQ for Marketing

**Q: How is this different from regular call transfers?**
> Regular transfers put customers on hold. Warm Transfer keeps them engaged with AI while connecting the human agent in the background.

**Q: Does the customer know they're being transferred?**
> Only when you want them to! The AI can announce the handoff naturally, or it can be seamless.

**Q: What if the agent doesn't answer?**
> The system detects this and the customer continues with the AI. No dropped calls, ever.

**Q: Can agents hear what the customer said to the AI?**
> Agents receive a custom briefing announcement. Full transcripts are also available in the dashboard.

**Q: How fast is the transfer?**
> From button click to agent connection: typically 10-15 seconds (time for agent to answer + hear briefing).
